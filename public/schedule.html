How to Schedule with Luber:

  1. You can group multiple vehicles under one schedule for the same day and time.
  2. You can also schedule individually to set a custom address per vehicle.
  3. You decide which services to include during scheduling (e.g., Engine or Cabin Air Filter).
  4. If you want different times for the same day, schedule them one at a time.
